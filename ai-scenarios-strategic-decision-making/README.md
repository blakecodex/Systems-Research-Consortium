# Pathways for the Digital Dollar
## A Three-Scenario Portfolio for CBDC Deployment Stress-Testing, 2026–2030

Graduate capstone for Georgetown's Certificate in AI Scenarios for Strategic
Decision-Making, directed by Dr. Frederic Lemieux.

The portfolio applies structured scenario planning to U.S. CBDC deployment
under conditions of irreducible uncertainty. The methodology is diagnostic
rather than predictive: scenarios are designed to expose the load-bearing
assumptions of the institutional consensus path, not to forecast a single
outcome.

## Three pathways

- **Alpha — Managed Transition.** Baseline. Phased rollout under sustained
  G10 coordination preserves commercial bank intermediation through 2030.
